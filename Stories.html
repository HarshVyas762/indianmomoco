The journey of The Indian Momo Company began with a shared passion for the culinary world and a profound love for the delightful treat - MOMOs.

Formed by Vibhanshu Mishra, the visionary founder, boasting of over 16 years of experience in the F&B industry, embarked on a mission to revolutionise the way MOMOs are savoured in India.

Drawing upon his extensive expertise in curating brands and launching F&B franchises globally, Vibhanshu identified a remarkable opportunity to introduce MOMOs in an organised and innovative fashion. His vision encompasses not only preserving the authentic flavours of this beloved dumpling but also elevating it to new heights through the infusion of freshness, fusion flavours, and innovative twists.

The Indian Momo Company stands as a brand that celebrates the rich traditions of MOMOs while simultaneously pushing the boundaries of creativity and taste. With a diverse range of offerings, it caters to the palates and preferences of individuals of all ages, delighting customers with a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation.

At The Indian Momo Company, we are committed to sharing the love of momos with people across India. That's why we offer franchising opportunities for our three outlet formats: The MOMOCle, MOMO Kiok and Single Outlet.
